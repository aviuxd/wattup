Who are we? Glad you asked.

We saw credit-worthy, responsible homeowners being turned away from solar installers and thought: "What's going on here?" Solar can save you money on your electricity bill, increase your property value, and help the planet. So, we decided to develop WattUp to make solar available to more people. With our innovative scoring system and capital connections, we hope to expand solar beyond those with pristine credit reports.
